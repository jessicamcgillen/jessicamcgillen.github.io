Welcome.

I'm Jessica McGillen, a postdoctoral researcher and consultant at Imperial College London working at the interface between disease modelling and global health policy.

I aim to understand large-scale patterns in global health investments and identify opportunities to improve their impact.

I love writing and see it as fundamental to good science and policy. At various points I've co-authored peer-reviewed publications, been a freelance editor, and written for a software company and a hospital.
